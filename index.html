Stories by Julie Hess


Gnome in the Night

By Julie Hess


A story about a gnome who suddenly leaves his house in search of wisdom. He meets a few friends along the way and in the end he learns something new about being wise.


Thor's Guide to Being a Hero

By Julie Hess



Thor My Hero is a Yorkshire Terrier whose superpower is to make you smile. The book is a collection of advice on what it takes to be a good hero given from the perspective of this little super dog.
